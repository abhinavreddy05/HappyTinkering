What we do?

At Happy Tinkering, we believe in the transformative power of innovation and the necessity of cultivating a mindset that embraces change. In a rapidly evolving world, we recognize the pivotal role of creativity, curiosity, and entrepreneurship in shaping the ‘future’

How we do?

Our suite of consulting services reflects our commitment to fostering innovation ecosystems and driving positive impact. Explore how we engage with our clients through the following specialized services

Innovation Strategy Consulting

Unleash the full potential of innovation within your organization. Our strategic consultants work closely with governments, systems, and organizations to craft and implement innovation strategies that drive qualitative and quantitative impact. Transform challenges into opportunities with our tailored solutions.

Makerspace and Tinkering Curriculum Design

Ignite a passion for creativity and hands-on learning. Our expert team designs makerspaces and tinkering curricula to foster innovation among students. From ideation to implementation, we create engaging environments that cultivate the next generation of problem solvers and innovators.

School Entrepreneurship Program

Empower the youth with entrepreneurial skills from an early age. Our School Entrepreneurship Program is designed to instill an entrepreneurial mindset, equipping students with the knowledge and skills needed to navigate the business world. Join us in shaping the leaders of tomorrow.
